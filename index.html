Projects

When Apps Lie: Uncovering the Real Cause Behind Transit App Stress

Why do transit apps lie? I conducted mixed-methods research, combining quantitative analysis, guerilla interviews, and ethnographic research, to identify the root causes of "ghost bus" anxiety and proposed a transparency-centered redesign.

User Research · Interviews · Data Analysis · Ethnographic Research

Comm(you)nity

A gamified mobile app designed to help users find their community by making real-time event discovery accessible. I led the end-to-end design of a personalized calendar and the task-based user testing process.

UI/UX · Product Design · User Testing · Competitive Analysis

Understanding Sense of Agency in Assistive Robotics

Is the fastest and most task-efficient robot actually the most helpful? I'm researching "sense of agency", the feeling of being in control over your own actions, in assistive tech by designing focus group protocol and a custom React/ROS control interface.

Focus Groups · User Interviews · UI/UX · HRI

Documenting Apple's AssistiveTouch

A two-month technical writing project dedicated to centralizing the fragmented history of Apple’s AssistiveTouch. By synthesizing academic papers and official documentation, I authored a peer-reviewed Wikipedia article to bridge a significant public knowledge gap for inclusive design.

Technical Writing · Qualitative Synthesis · Inclusive Design
